Introduction of General Situation and Background

  背景描述式（Introduction of General Situation and Background）是以叙事故事或时间为开头，从而引进作者对某种现象的观点或看法。背景描述式的开头，好处在于观点的提出是顺理成章的，便于文章的展开，对某种社会现象、倾向或社会问题进行深入剖析。
Example
  Nowadays, there is a prevailing phenomenon-young people are going after the trend and worship the stars. Some yong boys are self-content in having their hero’s name Beckham painted on the face. Still other fans even go far as to spend a huge amount of money–300 dollars on just a chic (=stylish) Backham hair-style. (From Fan)
