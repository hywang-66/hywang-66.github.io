(一) Numeration

Example
  A ready exmaple that comes to mind is SARS crsis that has recently torn apart and ravaged most of China. Admittedly, it had cost us quite a lot. Normal life had been completely turned upside down-offices are blocked, schools closed, shops and supermarkers and other public places deserted. Some people suffered from health damage and still even lost their lives and our economy suffers a temporary setback and a slowdown. It took quite a while for the country to recover from such a blow.

(二) Exmaplification

  举例扩展(Examplification)是指用具体的事例说明人或事物的关系、本质及规律，可是使抽象的概念变得具体生动。但所选事例必须与所讲主题紧密相关，要其具有代表性、典型性，说服力强。具体来说，举例扩展可以使事实和数据、方式或方法，也可以是原因或结果，举例扩展必须选取事例来说明。
  举例推展的常用表达有:

  • For example, …
  • For instance, …
  • Take … for example.
  • Take … as an example.
  • Our life abounds with examples.
  • History/This nation/Our society abounds with examples of …
  • A case in point is …
  • The truth in the definition goes for/is applicable to many cases in our life.
  • I can think of no better illustration of this idea than the example …

Example
  Rather than “stuning” man’s imagination, the “overuse” of modern technologies actually enables man to be more and more creative. For instance, Dream Works, the famous American film company, has been designing and producing catroons using computer technology.

They, next, give us energy to continue our work and studies as well as incentive and momentum to create. (七) Listing of Effects

Example

One such problem is the ever-increasing pressure on urban facilities, including trasport, housing, and water supply. A second problem is the physical environment. Because of high concentration of population, people have to face much more serious problems of waste gas and polluted water. Of all our problems, the most serious and pressing one is concerned with the widespread crime due to the increased mobility.

(八) Extension

  延长因果连(Extension)是原因推展和结果推展的综合延伸，指的是就某一因果链，可以往前、中、后延长。
Example
  Stess stems from the fierce competition among colleagues. Every person aspires to success. This very desire pushes people to scramble for limited positions and resources at all costs, creating intense pressure on everyone.

(九) Concession

  让步扩展(Concession)实际上是逆向思维在推展段落中的具体运用，从反映切入主题，另辟蹊径，从而达到论证主题思想的作用。让步推展的常用表达有:

  • Adimittedly …
  • It is undeniable …
  • There is no denying the fact that …
  • Some people think that …, because… However, … In fact …
  • … are often criticized because … The evidence is that this sitation has improved considerably in recently years.
  • There is a view held by some people that … However …
  • There is a widespread belief that … The example of … refutes that …

Example
  There is no denying the fact that if the price of fuel were increased, the use of cars would be restricted. However, this policy would also bring chaos in our society. One possible consequence we would probably face is the anger among the public. For example, taxi drivers would work more time to support their cost. And if they often work under such pressure, they would cause serious traffic accidents.

Example

They would be no intellectual honesty and people would no longer believe what the scientists say. Since scientists are noble elites of society, if their words are incredible, then the crediblity of the whole society would deteriorate and collapse.

(十一) Definition

  定义推展(Definition)通常给说明对象一个明确的概念，解释事物的本质特征，勾勒起大概，确定事物的范围和界限，从而使抽象难懂的事物或概念变得清楚、具体。定义推展分为正式和非正式两种方式，正式定义指给一个事物下一个科学正式的定义，往往需要求助于字典或百科全书这种权威的参考书。而非正式定义，只要根据个人的看法下定义即可。定义推展的常用看法有:

  • 名词: meaning, definition, sense
  • 动词: mean, define, be said, be supposed
  • 句式:
    … is often referred to as …
    … is usually defined as …
    To most people, the definition of … is …
    By saying …, we mean …
    The term … is used in various senses, not all derived from …
    If adopted to …, it can come in different meanings …
    If it is intended for …, a new meaning to it begins to stand out.

Exmaple
  Ambition is the decision one makes and the resolution with which he carries out that decision. It provides us with the required driving force to accomplish any undertaking in our life. Just as Joseph Epstein, a famous American writer put it, “And as we decide and choose, so are our lives formed.” Indeed, once we make up our decision, our life becomes meaningful and specially orientated. This nation of life, as far as I observe, is closet to the truth and does apply to almost all aspects of life.

Exmaple
  Nothing seems more important than to maintain a good health. With physical fitness, we can do almost anying that we wish to fulfill ourselves. Without strong constitution, we can do almsot nothing no matter what aspirations we have.

(十三) Analogy

  类比推展是把两个类似的物品放在一起，从而使一些深奥难懂的主题变得更加直观、更容易处理和把握，增强论证的效果和说服力。与类比推展对应的是(十四)中的对比推展(Constrast),对比推展主要强调两个比较对象的不同点。类比推展的表达有：

  • … By the same token, …
  • … In the same manner, …
  • … Similarly …
  • … Just as …, so …
  • … too
  • A is to B what C is to D.

Exmaple
  Crsis is to our life what illness is to our body. Just as fatal diseases can destory our body and sometimes even put an end to our lives, so does smashing crsis knock us down so irrevocably and hopelessly that we might never have chance of standing up again. However, most crises in life do not fall into such a category. These crises, like occasional ailments which sometimes can bolster our body’s immune system, are mostly surmountable and therefore making us hardy enough to survive whatever adversities in our later life. Even those serious crises, if handled properly, can be turned to a blessing to us, too.

(十四) Contrast

  对比推展(Contrast)的表达有:

  • … On the contrary, …
  • … By contrast, …
  • Contrary to …
  • A is everything B is not.
  • A is different from B in almost all aspects.
  • In the past, …, but nowadays, …
  • Conversely, …

Most of them will send part of theri income back hoome to help their families. What’s more, these farmers will help develp the cities in many ways. According to a recent survey, most of the construction projects in big cities are actually done by farmers. By contrast, some people think that farmer migration may give rise to series of negative effects. They worry that much land will be deerted in the country. In addition, it is also belived that rising crimes in cities are partly related to this migration.

Example

(十六) Divison and Classification

  分类推展(Division and Classification), 是指将事物按照一定的标准和范围，分成若干点或若干方面来谈，逻辑方式很分明，从不同层次进行论证。不过，在进行分类推展时，要注意分类的标准与原则的统一性，避免各类别之间的重叠。分类推展的常用表达有：

  • 名词：types, kinds, aspects, groups, classes, sorts, qualities, characteristics, categories
  • 动词及动词短语：sort, divide, classify, categorize, group, organize, put into, fall into, palce into, include
  • 句式：
    … consisting of … according to …
    … comprises … on the basis of …
    … may be classified depending up/on…
    The classification is based up/on …

Example
  One of the strangest features of motherhood is that the vast majority of mothers prefer to cradle their babies in the left arm. Why should this be? The obvious explanation is that the majority of mothers are right-handed and they wish to keep their righ hands free. Unfortunately, this explanation cannot apply, because left-handed mothers also favor their left arms for holding their babies. The precise figures are 83 percent for right-handed mothers and 78 percent for left-handed mothers.
